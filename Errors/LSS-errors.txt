<nierror code="403560">
Malformed Style Sheet.  The LSS is not formatted properly and cannot be interpreted.  See Detailed Help or Getting Started Guide for details on formatting LSS.
</nierror>
<nierror code="403561">
The Rules String references a control by name that cannot be found on the front panel.  Rename the control, or change the Rules string to match.
